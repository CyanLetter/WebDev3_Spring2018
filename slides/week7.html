Hosting Your Website

To put your site out on the web you need:

  • A Server
  • A Domain Name

What is a server?

A computer with a permanent IP address that you can throw your files onto.

IP Address

  • Every computer has one
  • Servers are accessibile from anywhere
  • Difference between local and external IP

Public / External IP

  • Assigned by your ISP
  • How your computer communicates with google, your website, etc.

Private / Local IP

  • Assigned by your router
  • How your router communicates between your ISP and your computers
  • Not externally visible

ICANN

Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers.

Oversees things like IP address and domain name registry.

What is a domain name?

A human readable address that you register with an ICANN registrar.

ICANN process diagram.

Domains must

  • Be registered to a person
  • Point to a specific server

See also: WHOIS

DNS

Servers that keep a log of nameservers for all domain names

Nameservers

Hosting provider servers that connect you to a specific server IP
