Week 6

SEO

SEO

Search Engine Optimization,

or improving your website's ranking in unpaid search results.

This effectively means Google.

Things change fast

Search engines constantly tweak their rules.

Old SEO tactics can now hurt you ranking.

A broad field

There are experts who focus on this exclusively.

This is a good primer.

This takes time

Changing a site and seeing results is an iterative process.

The Fundamentals

  • Unique Titles
  • Original Content
  • Good User Experience

Good user experience

Google wants a good experience for its users.

Good UX means showing their users...

  • Relevant content
  • Reputable sources
  • Easily navigable sites
  • Mobile first sites

SEO is not...

  • Cheating the system
  • Useless filler
  • Spammy or ad-heavy sites

Crawling and Indexing

How search engines index the web.

Google's explanation.

Crawlers

A web filled with spiders.

Bots periodically go to webpages, look for links, and follow them.

robots.txt

A file telling crawlers what to ignore


User-agent: *
Disallow: /nobots/

Sitemap: http://www.example.com/sitemap.xml

Indexing

Bots scrape the site and attempt to record its contents.

Search

Google attempts to pull together relevant pieces in its search results.

Tools

Ways to check how well your site shapes up.

Google Webmaster Tools

Free, requires an actual site with proof of ownership.

Google Pagespeed Insights

Test speed and relevance on mobile and desktop.

SEO Tester

Check more detailed SEO recommendations.

Metadata

Meta tags that affect SEO

Title Tag

  • First line of results
  • Should be unique per page
  • Should match a page's topic

Starbucks is a good example.

Meta Description

A summary of the page, longer than the title.

Likely to be used directly in the result.

Keywords

This is no longer used.

Platform Specific

Additional control over your site's presentation

  • Facebook's Open Graph
  • Google's Structured Data
  • Other Microdata

Content

"Make your site interesting and useful" - Google

H tag structure

  • One h1, two - three h2s, etc.
  • Like a page outline
  • Determines keyword importance

The fold

Above the fold content should be immediately useful.

Ads can hurt this.

User Generated Content

Spam comments can hurt your ranking

Links

URL

Use words in your urls

Internal links

Define a clear hierarchy and site flow, starting from the root.

Make links available immediately.

External Links

  • Link to reputable sites
  • Get linked to by reputable sites
  • Use nofollow

Mobile First
